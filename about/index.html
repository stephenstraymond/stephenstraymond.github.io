  • Personal Life

  • Born in Bridgeport, CT in 1996, Stephen lived most of his life in a small town called Easton, with his parents, Steve and Melanie, and older sister, Kathryn. As a kid, Stephen developed an interest in outdoor sports, such as skiing and mountain biking, both of which he continues to persue today. In addition to adventure sports, Stephen spent much of his childhood playing for various local sports teams. Stephen played baseball for most of elementary and middle school, and joined the local football team for several years in middle school. Though he no longer plays for a team, Stephen continues follow both the NFL and MLS, and supports both the New Orleans Saints and the New York City Football Club.

  • Stephen's interest in engineering began when he was just a kid. Growing up, Stephen's favorite toys were LEGOs. Now he's an engineer. Crazy, right?
